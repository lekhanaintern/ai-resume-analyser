02 — Service
Description of Service

AI ResumeAnalyzer provides AI-powered career tools to help job seekers optimise their resumes and prepare for interviews. Our ATS Score Analysis evaluates your resume on a 0–100 scale across nine dimensions including contact information, section structure, action verbs, formatting, and quantified achievements. Our AI Resume Rewriting engine uses natural language processing to rewrite and tailor your resume for specific job roles by injecting role-relevant keywords and strong action verbs.

The platform's Role Prediction feature uses a trained machine learning model to predict your top three best-fit career paths based on your resume content and skills. Our AI Mock Interview tool generates role-specific multiple choice questions tailored to your predicted job role, scores your responses, and provides performance insights to help you prepare for real interviews. Finally, our PDF Export feature allows you to download your optimised resume as a professionally formatted, ATS-friendly document.
